My name is Randy Bauer, I'm an Idaho native born and raised in Caldwell. At 11 years old I started learning to play the trumpet; I've now been playing the trumpet for 15 years. My experience as a young trumpet player was difficult because my embouchure and fundementals were poorly established. No matter how many hours I practiced, I played with pain in my embourchure and struggled to perform. Despite these challenges I persevered and underwent an embouchure change my first year of college.

Through my experiences as a Trumpet Performance Major at Boise State University I corrected my embouchure and sharpened my fundementals. While I changed how I played, I discovered that I wanted to help other trumpet players develop proper technique so they wouldn't endure the same struggles I had faced. I will always be grateful for the journey I went through, from starting college re-learning how to play trumpet, to winning the concerto aria with the BSU Orchestra my final year, and meeting my wife Grace. When I'm not playing trumpet or teaching I enjoy working on cars, camping, and trying out new recipes in the kitchen.

Give each of my students a strong foundation in fundementals and proper playing technique.

Build a individual practice routine for each student based off their performance goals and skill level.

Instil problem solving skills by teaching each student how to identify weakness in their playing and what exercises to use to strengthen their weaknesses.

My greatest goal is to play with LA Philharmonic. ect.

More about my Experience

Randy is well versed in both classical and jazz trumpet styles and has a reputation of excellence in all areas of music. He holds a Bachelor of Music in Trumpet Performance and is currently pursuing a Master of Music in Trumpet Performance from Boise State University. During his time at Boise State, Mr. Bauer has taught private lessons masterclasses in the Treasure Valley to students across all ages and varying levels of experience. Mr. Bauer is committed to maintaining a versatile approach to trumpet performance practice and pedagogy and sharing this approach with his trumpet studio.
