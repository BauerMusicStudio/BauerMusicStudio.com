About Me

Hi, my name is Randy Bauer. I'm an Idaho native and hold a Master's Degree in Trumpet Performance from Boise State University. I began my private lesson studio in 2017 and have over 15 years of experience honing my artistry. I have studio locations in Boise and Eagle, and also offer online trumpet lessons.
