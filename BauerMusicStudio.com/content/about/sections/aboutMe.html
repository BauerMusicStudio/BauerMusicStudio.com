My name is Randy Bauer, I'm an Idaho native. I've been honing my trumpet skills for over 15 years. My experience as a young trumpet player was difficult because my embouchure was poorly established.Despite the hours I practiced, I played with pain in my embouchure and struggled to perform. I persevered and decided to change my embouchure my first year of college.

As a Trumpet Performance Major at Boise State University I corrected my embouchure and sharpened my fundamentals. I also found my passion for helping other trumpet players develop proper technique to help them avoid the same mistakes I struggled through.

When I'm not playing trumpet or teaching I enjoy working on cars, camping, and trying out new recipes in the kitchen with my wife.

Give each of my students a strong foundation in fundamentals and proper playing technique.

Build an individual practice routine for each student based on their performance goals and skill level.

Instil problem solving skills by teaching each student how to identify weakness in their playing and what exercises to use to strengthen their weaknesses.

My greatest goal is to play with the LA Philharmonic.

More about my Experience

Randy is well versed in both classical and jazz trumpet styles and has a reputation of excellence in all areas of music. He holds a Bachelor of Music in Trumpet Performance and is currently pursuing a Master of Music in Trumpet Performance from Boise State University. During his time at Boise State, Mr. Bauer has taught private lessons masterclasses in the Treasure Valley to students across all ages and varying levels of experience.
